Welcome to Soccer Sphere

Project Overview

Soccer Sphere is a distributed football league management system for campus tournaments. It includes four subsystems: Match Management, handling scheduling, results, and live updates; Financial Management, managing registration fees and reports; User Management, overseeing accounts and roles; and Team Management, organizing teams and statistics. Built with Spring Boot, React, and MySQL, it ensures scalable football event organization. It can greatly improve the efficiency of match organizers and reduce the possibility of errors.
